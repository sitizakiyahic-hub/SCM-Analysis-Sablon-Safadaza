# SCM-Analysis-Sablon-Safadaza

## Overview
This repository contains an academic group project developed for the Supply Chain Management course at Telkom University

The project focuses on analyzing the supply chain activities of Sablon Safadaza, a local custom t-shirt printing business.

The analysis covers procurement, operation, inventory management, distribution, and marketing and sales processes. Based on the identified issues, improvement strategis were proposed to enhance operasional efficiency and business performance.

## Project Objectives

- Analyze the current Supply Chain Management (SCM) processes.
- Identify operational challenges and root causes.
- Evaluate the impact of business problems.
- Propose practical recommendations for business improvement.

## Scope of Analysis

- Procurement
- Operations
- Inventory Management
- Distribution
- Marketing and Sales

## Tools and Methods

- Microsoft Word
- Microsoft Excel
- Fishbone Diagram (Cause and Effect Analysis)
- Business Peocess Analysis
- Literatur Review
- Interview

## Key Findings
The analysis identified several challenges, including:

- Manual inventory recording system.
- Limited production capacity due to a single press machine.
- Limited workforce during peak demand.
- Underutilized digital marketing strategies.
- Dependence on supplier stock availability.

## Proposed Solutions
Several improvement strategies were recommended, such as:

- Digital communication with suppliers.
- Additional production equipment.
- Workforce expansion.
- Digital inventory recording using Microsoft Excel.
- Enhanced marketing through social media and e-commerce platforms.
